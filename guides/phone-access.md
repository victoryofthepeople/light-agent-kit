# Phone Access

Phone access means you can message your assistant from your phone.

This is useful for quick notes, voice thoughts, task capture, and check-ins.

## Start Here

Ask:

> Do I actually need phone access yet?

Good reasons:

- You capture thoughts while walking.
- You want quick daily check-ins.
- You want to send voice notes.
- You want reminders or planning away from your desk.

## Options

### Use Your AI Tool's Mobile App

This is the simplest path when available.

Good for:

- Chatting with the assistant.
- Sharing notes.
- Asking for plans.

### Use A Telegram-Style Bot

This can be useful for a custom assistant that receives messages and voice notes.

Good for:

- A dedicated assistant chat.
- Voice note capture.
- Background reminders.

This is more advanced. Add it after your core file system works.

### Stay Desktop-Only

This is a strong starting choice.

Good for:

- Keeping setup simple.
- Learning the system before adding more access.

## Safety Notes

- Treat phone messages as private.
- Keep secrets out of phone chat.
- Decide where transcripts are stored.
- Keep clear yes rules clear before adding automation.
